Research Interests

I am interested in how learned representations behave under real-world data conditions and how evaluation choices shape our understanding of model reliability.

  • How do representation instabilities emerge under long-tail data regimes?
    Sparse interaction patterns and minority classes may produce geometrically unstable embeddings that standard metrics fail to detect.
  • When do aggregate evaluation metrics mask decision-level risk?
    High overall accuracy can hide systematic failures in rare but critical cases, particularly under class imbalance or distribution shift.
  • What internal model signals indicate emerging failures before degradation appears in aggregate metrics?
    Attention fragmentation, embedding variance, and per-class diagnostics may serve as early warning signals.
  • How do data quality irregularities propagate through analytical and decision systems?
    Missing values, measurement error, and temporal drift can compound through modeling pipelines in ways that aren't captured by static validation.
