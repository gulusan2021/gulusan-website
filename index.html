About

I am a machine learning and data science professional with experience building models and analytics for complex operational and customer-facing settings. My work includes customer behavior modeling, sentiment analysis using transformer-based NLP models, time series forecasting, dimensionality reduction (PCA, t-SNE), and quantitative segmentation.

I originally trained in business and management and hold an MBA. My master’s research examined structural barriers to career advancement for women managers through survey-based empirical analysis. That work introduced me to system-level thinking, feedback loops, and how metrics and structures shape outcomes—ideas that continue to influence how I approach machine learning and data systems today.

In recent years I have completed applied data science and cloud training through MIT Professional Education and Great Learning, and I am building a portfolio that bridges hands-on ML work with broader questions about robustness, governance, and decision impact.

Selected Projects

Examples illustrating my experience with representation learning, evaluation, and applied ML in realistic settings.

Representation & Modeling

  • SVD-Based Recommendation Systems
    Python • Surprise Library • SVD • Collaborative Filtering

    Implemented SVD-based collaborative filtering and rank-based recommenders on user–item interaction data. Handled sparsity, tuned hyperparameters, and evaluated performance using precision@k, recall@k, and RMSE.

    Helped build intuition about how different representations and data regimes influence model behavior.
  • CNN vs. ANN for Digit Recognition
    TensorFlow • CNN • ANN • MNIST

    Built and compared convolutional and fully connected networks for digit classification, analyzing generalization and robustness under different training regimes and architecture depths.

  • Dimensionality Reduction & Latent Structure
    PCA • t-SNE • clustering • visualization

    Applied PCA and t-SNE to high-dimensional data, interpreting eigenstructure, variance explained, and cluster separation via silhouette scores and visual inspection.

NLP, Imbalanced Data & Pipelines

  • Sentiment Analysis with Transformer Models
    Hugging Face Transformers • NLP • Sentiment

    Built sentiment classifiers on customer text using transformer-based models. Focused on preprocessing, fine-tuning, and evaluation using accuracy, F1, and confusion matrices with targeted error analysis.

  • Predictive Modeling Under Class Imbalance
    Python • Imbalanced data • ROC-AUC • F1

    Developed models for imbalanced prediction problems (e.g., approval risk, cancellation risk) using resampling strategies and threshold tuning. Evaluated performance via ROC-AUC, precision–recall, and F1 scores.

  • End-to-End Workflows in Azure ML
    Azure ML • Experiment tracking • MLOps

    Deployed ML workflows with reproducible experiments, configuration management, and integration into existing analytics environments.
